Tinted Permit

Tinted Permit

Tinted glass permit allows the car owner to use tinted glass legally. This condition of dimmed car windows, which cuts down visibility into the car, is known as glass tint. In Nigeria, for a number of official reasons, vehicle owners require an official tint permit in order to drive a car with tinted glass legally. NOTE: TINTED PERMIT IS ISSUED TO MOTOR VEHICLE(S) WITH FACTORY-FITTED TINTED GLASS ONLY. If otherwise, then you’ll need a doctors report
